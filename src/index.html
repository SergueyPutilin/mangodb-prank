The new Golden standard

MangoDB is the new golden standard for your database solutions in NodeJS. Outperforming all of the competition such as Mongo(L)DB, Postgresql, regular PHP databases and many more while being just as reliable. MangoDB is already widely used by the industry and provides a stable and fruity developing platform for all kinds of applications like:

  • Farming spreadsheet applications to calculate farm layouts
  • Content management systems => See FCM, Fruity Content Manager
  • Many more!

With a lot of community support we can safely say that MangoDB will be maintained for a very long time with a lot of new features already on the roadmap.

Fruity syntax

MangoDB has a F R U I T Y~~~ ♩♪ syntax inspired by the world of agriculture. We decided to change most of the Database terms to something related to agriculture. E.g. Databases are Farms, tables are Fields and columns are Crops. To work your way around the code, we also changed a lot of method terms:

  • PLANT: Add items
  • PICK: Get items
  • CURE: Patch items
  • TOTHECOMPOSTBIN: Delete items
  • Many more!

We hope that developers (or as we call them: Farmers) will be able to smoothly transition from other Database frameworks because of our F R U I T Y~~~ ♩♪ syntax and hope they'll get their 99 Farming skilling cape in no time!

Features

MangoDB has a lot of great features that will make think to yourself "why didn't I use MangoDB before!". Here is a list of some of our amazing features:

  • Grows new data naturally (idk it just does)
  • Photosynthesis, the more data, the more it produces new available space for new data.
  • NoSQL: You have to do it manually, just like on the farm!
  • Composter: Make queries grow faster
  • Easy DB dumps: You can dig it out and plant the db somewhere else without any data loss.
